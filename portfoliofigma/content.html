OVERVIEW

The idea of ​​making the Digibook is expected that the concept will increase the interest of everyone, especially young people, in reading. My goal was to develop this new platform to be more user-friendly, fun, and interactive and increase interest in reading with the Digibook platform.

BACKGROUND

Based on a survey conducted by the Program for International Student Assessment (PISA) released by the Organization for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD) in 2021, Indonesia ranks 62 out of 70 countries, or is the bottom 10 countries with low literacy levels. From the survey, Indonesian people especially young people can be categorized as lazy reading.thus i ask myself:

“ HOW MIGHT WE MAKE READING ACTIVITIES MORE INTERACTIVE AND FUN”

CORE EXPERIENCES

While we were going through the process of designing Digibook, we learned that there are other apps that also provide ebooks. Although not exclusive, we want to create a better reading experience that can increase reading interest, especially for young people. For a more in-depth discussion of our creative process, scroll down to the next section!

AUDIOBOOK

For some people reading is an activity that can only be done when they are free.

With the Audiobook feature, reading can be done anytime and anywhere during your busy activities.when you can't read them, hear them. your favorite book is always in your pocket.

READING GOALS

It makes you even more excited to read and discover things when you are fascinated with statistics.

Reading Goals let you keep tracking your reading activity. set your goals, finish more books, and earn points to get discounts.

SUMMARY

When you finish reading a book, you want to understand the content of the chapter you have read, but feel lazy to read it again.

The Summary feature helps you remind the contents of the reading without having to reread all the chapters

RESEARCH

In this step, we delved deeper into users' needs, and business competitors to discover and understand this problem space in more dept. Due to time constraints, user research was conducted via survey with 18 respond.

Main finding:

  • Respondents more often read through the internet
  • Respondents are lazy to read because they feel bored quickly
  • Respondents said they like to read books casually

STYLE GUIDE

Purple is rare in nature, making purple is often perceived with imagination, spirituality, and a mysterious side.That’s why the use of purple is considered to attract attention.Thus, we chose purple as the product's primary branding color, while the other brighter secondary colors add excitement and life to the interface and give it a fun personality.

USER TESTING

I created 5 different tasks and scenarios for each usability test to understand how users would interact with our product. I ask the user about the overall level of process flow in the context of design and convenience with a Single Ease Question. Overall, the feedback was positive, and we were able to implement several changes to improve our product:

Comments: Overall the design is good, easy to use, but several pieces of information need to be improved, especially on the reading book screen.

Sugestions: The pagination is better by using a button number (1,2,3,4) rather than using “page 121 of 200” and giving us the information on the page where we are on.
